The adult race includes a 400m pool based swim (17 lengths at Hatch End swimming {links.pool}) followed by a 17km road based {links.adult_cycle.cycle} (3 laps) and an off-road 3km {links.adult_run.run} (3 laps at Hatch End playing {links.fields}). Junior races {links.junior_cycle_run.cycle} on the playing {links.fields} rather than the roads with age-appropriate adjustments to distances(see table below).
