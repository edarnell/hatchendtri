The adult race includes a 400m pool based swim (17 lengths at Hatch End swimming {link.pool}) followed by a 17km road based {link.adult_cycle.cycle} (3 laps) and an off-road 3km {link.adult_run.run} (3 laps at Hatch End playing {link.fields}). Junior races {link.junior_cycle_run.cycle} on the playing {link.fields} rather than the roads with age-appropriate adjustments to distances(see table below).
