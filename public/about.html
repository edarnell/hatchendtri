Hatch End Harrow Triathlon

The Hatch End – Harrow Triathlon is a popular race for people wanting a new challenge. The pool based swim and less competitive, family-oriented nature makes this an ideal starter race for those new to the sport.

The Adult race features a 400m pool swim, 17km road cycle and 3km off road run. Junior races are for ages 8-16, with an off road cycle and British Triathlon approved age appropriate distances.

Hatch End Triathlon is organised and run by Jetstream Triathlon Club. The event is approved by British Triathlon run according to BTF rules.
